Quick Questions?

Check out our most frequently asked questions

How quickly can you complete my transcription?

Standard turnaround is 48 hours, with express (24 hours) and rush (same day) options available for urgent projects.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept all major audio and video formats including MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, and more. Maximum file size is 500MB.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

All files are encrypted during transfer and storage. Our team signs strict NDAs and we're HIPAA compliant for medical transcription.

What are your pricing rates?

Pricing starts at $2.00 per audio minute for general transcription, with specialized services priced based on complexity.
