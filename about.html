About JD Legal Transcripts

Your trusted partner in professional transcription services since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by legal professional Jennifer Davis, JD Legal Transcripts was born from a simple observation: the legal industry needed more accurate, secure, and timely transcription services. After years of working with unreliable transcription providers, Jennifer decided to create a company that would set new standards for quality and confidentiality.

What started as a small operation serving local law firms has grown into a trusted partner for legal professionals, medical institutions, and corporate clients across the country. Our commitment to precision, security, and client satisfaction has remained unwavering throughout our journey.

Today, we proudly serve over 500 clients nationwide, maintaining the same attention to detail and personal service that defined our early days.
