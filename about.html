About JD Legal Transcripts

Your trusted partner in professional transcription services, committed to accuracy, confidentiality, and exceptional client service since 2015.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by experienced legal professionals, JD Legal Transcripts emerged from a clear need in the market for reliable, accurate, and confidential transcription services specifically tailored to the legal and medical industries.

What started as a small team of certified transcriptionists has grown into a trusted nationwide service provider, handling thousands of hours of audio content monthly while maintaining our commitment to quality and security.

Today, we serve law firms, medical practices, corporations, and academic institutions across the United States, providing them with the precision and reliability they demand for their critical documentation needs.
