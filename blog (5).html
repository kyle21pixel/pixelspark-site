5 Free Ways to Market Your Business Online

Getting started with online marketing doesn’t have to be expensive. Here are 5 powerful strategies you can use today:

  1. Optimize your website for search engines (SEO).
  2. Start a free blog to build your brand’s voice and trust.
  3. Use Canva to create beautiful and shareable social media posts.
  4. Leverage Facebook and WhatsApp groups to reach niche communities.
  5. Ask happy customers to leave you Google or social reviews.
