Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our transcription services, processes, and policies.

We specialize in four main types of transcription services: Legal Transcription (court proceedings, depositions, hearings), Medical Transcription (patient records, medical reports, HIPAA compliant), Interview & Zoom Meeting Transcription (video conferences, focus groups, interviews), and General & Academic Transcription (research interviews, corporate meetings, podcasts). Each service is handled by certified professionals with specialized expertise in that field.

We offer three turnaround options to meet your needs:

  • Standard (48-72 hours): Our most economical option with guaranteed quality
  • Rush (24 hours): Perfect for urgent projects that need quick turnaround
  • Same Day (8-12 hours): Emergency service for critical time-sensitive documents

Delivery times start from when we receive your complete project details and payment confirmation.

We accept virtually all audio and video file formats, including:

Audio formats:
  • MP3, WAV, M4A
  • FLAC, OGG, WMA
  • AIFF, AMR, AAC
Video formats:
  • MP4, AVI, MOV
  • WMV, FLV, MKV
  • WEBM, 3GP, ASF

Maximum file size is 2GB per file. If you have a different format, contact us and we'll work with you to find a solution.

Our pricing is based on three main factors:

  • Audio Length: Calculated per minute of audio/video content
  • Complexity: Technical terminology, multiple speakers, audio quality affect pricing
  • Turnaround Time: Standard, rush, or same-day delivery options

Typical rates range from $1.50-$6.00 per audio minute depending on these factors. We provide detailed quotes before starting any work, and there are never any hidden fees.

  • • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)

  • • Purchase orders accepted from government agencies and large corporations

All payments are processed securely with bank-level encryption. We require 50% deposit for new clients, with the balance due upon delivery.

Confidentiality is our top priority. We implement multiple layers of security:

  • • All staff sign comprehensive Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs)
  • • 256-bit SSL encryption for all file transfers and storage
  • • HIPAA compliance for medical transcription services
  • • Secure, access-controlled facilities with background-checked employees
  • • Files are automatically deleted from our servers after 30 days
  • • ISO 27001 certified information security management

We can also provide additional security measures such as custom NDAs and on-site transcription for highly sensitive projects.

Yes, we are fully HIPAA compliant for all medical transcription services. Our HIPAA compliance includes signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs), encrypted file storage and transmission, access controls, audit trails, staff training on healthcare privacy regulations, and secure disposal of all materials. Our medical transcriptionists are specifically trained in HIPAA requirements and medical terminology. We regularly undergo compliance audits to ensure we maintain the highest standards of healthcare data protection.

Our quality assurance process ensures 99.9% accuracy through multiple checkpoints:

  1. 1. Initial Transcription: Performed by certified transcriptionists with relevant expertise
  2. 2. First Review: Senior transcriptionist reviews for accuracy and formatting
  3. 3. Quality Check: Dedicated proofreader performs final accuracy verification
  4. 4. Technical Review: Final check for formatting, timestamps, and client specifications
  5. 5. Client Satisfaction: We offer free revisions if any issues are found

This multi-level process ensures exceptional accuracy while meeting your deadline requirements.
