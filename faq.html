General Questions

We offer four main types of transcription services:

  • Legal Transcription: Court proceedings, depositions, arbitrations, legal interviews
  • Medical Transcription: Patient consultations, medical reports, clinical notes (HIPAA compliant)
  • Interview & Zoom Transcription: Virtual meetings, job interviews, research interviews
  • General & Academic: Lectures, podcasts, focus groups, training sessions

We offer three turnaround options:

  • Standard (48 hours): Perfect for most projects with standard quality requirements
  • Express (24 hours): Priority processing for urgent projects (additional cost)
  • Rush (Same day): Emergency service for critical deadlines (premium cost)

Turnaround times are calculated from the time we receive your complete files and project details, not including weekends and holidays.

We guarantee 99% accuracy or better on all transcriptions. Our quality control process includes:

  • Professional transcriptionists with specialized training
  • Multi-level quality review process
  • Specialized knowledge in legal, medical, and technical terminology
  • Free revisions if accuracy falls below our standards

Yes, we provide speaker identification for all multi-speaker recordings. We can:

  • Identify speakers by voice characteristics (Speaker 1, Speaker 2, etc.)
  • Use provided names if you give us speaker information
  • Add timestamps to track when each speaker begins talking
  • Format conversations clearly for easy reading

Pricing & Billing

Our pricing is based on audio length and service type:

  • General & Academic: Starting at $2.00 per audio minute
  • Interview & Zoom: Starting at $2.25 per audio minute
  • Legal Transcription: Starting at $2.50 per audio minute
  • Medical Transcription: Starting at $2.75 per audio minute

Express and rush services have additional fees. Volume discounts available for large projects.

We calculate costs based on the total audio duration, not file size. Here's how it works:

  • We measure the actual speaking time in your recording
  • Silent periods and pauses are not charged
  • Multiple speakers don't increase the per-minute rate
  • You receive a detailed quote before we begin work
  • No hidden fees or surprise charges

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal and PayPal Business accounts
  • Bank transfers (ACH) for corporate clients
  • Invoicing with NET 30 terms for established clients
  • Purchase orders for government and institutional clients

Yes, we offer volume discounts based on project size:

  • 10+ hours: 5% discount
  • 25+ hours: 10% discount
  • 50+ hours: 15% discount
  • 100+ hours: 20% discount

We also offer special rates for academic institutions, non-profits, and long-term contracts. Contact us for a custom quote.

Technical & Files

We accept all major audio and video formats:

Audio Formats:

  • MP3, WAV, M4A
  • AAC, FLAC, OGG
  • WMA, AIFF

Video Formats:

  • MP4, MOV, AVI
  • MKV, WMV, FLV
  • WebM, 3GP

Our file upload system has the following limitations:

  • Maximum file size: 500MB per individual file
  • Total upload limit: 2GB per project submission
  • For larger files: We can provide secure FTP access or accept files via cloud storage links

If you have files larger than these limits, please contact us for alternative upload methods.

For best transcription results, we recommend:

  • Bit rate: At least 128 kbps for audio files
  • Sample rate: 44.1 kHz or higher
  • Background noise: Minimize background noise when possible
  • Microphone: Use external microphones for better quality than built-in mics
  • Recording environment: Quiet rooms with minimal echo

Don't worry if your audio isn't perfect - our team is skilled at working with challenging audio quality.

We provide transcripts in multiple formats:

  • Microsoft Word (.docx): Fully editable with formatting
  • PDF: Professional, print-ready documents
  • Plain Text (.txt): Simple, universal format
  • Excel Spreadsheet: For structured data projects
  • Custom formats: We can accommodate special requirements

Security & Privacy

We take confidentiality very seriously with multiple security measures:

  • Encryption: All files are encrypted during upload, storage, and transmission
  • NDAs: Every team member signs strict non-disclosure agreements
  • Access control: Files are only accessible to assigned transcriptionists
  • Secure deletion: Files are permanently deleted after project completion
  • HIPAA compliance: Full compliance for medical transcription projects

Yes, we are fully HIPAA compliant for all medical transcription services:

  • Business Associate Agreements (BAA) available
  • HIPAA-trained transcriptionists for medical content
  • Secure, encrypted infrastructure
  • Audit trails for all file access and modifications
  • Regular compliance audits and training updates

Our file retention policy is designed for security and convenience:

  • Active projects: Files kept securely until project completion
  • Completed projects: Files retained for 30 days after delivery for revisions
  • After 30 days: All files are permanently and securely deleted
  • Extended retention: Available upon request for legal or compliance needs
  • Legal projects: Can be retained longer per attorney requirements

We have comprehensive security protocols in place:

  • Prevention: Multiple layers of security prevent unauthorized access
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and intrusion detection
  • Response plan: Immediate notification and response procedures
  • Client notification: Immediate contact if any breach affects your data
  • Legal compliance: Full compliance with breach notification laws
